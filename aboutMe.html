Welcome to Zaun
Zaun is a city known for its unrestrained innovation and technological advancements, but at the cost of pollution, corruption, and social divide. The streets are filled with steam vents, towering factories, and a populace that thrives in the city's darker corners. The people of Zaun are resourceful and often ruthless, constantly innovating through the chaos to push the boundaries of science and technology. Here, the line between science and madness is thin, and many Zaunites walk it daily.
